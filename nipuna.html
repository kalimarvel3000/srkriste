NIPUNA

WELCOME TO NIPUNA PAGE!

NIPUNA, the National Level Techno- Management Symposium, organized by the ISTE student chapter at SRKREC for almost three decades, stands as a symbol of excellence and innovation. Students eagerly participate in this annual event, utilizing the dynamic platform to showcase their unique talents and capabilities. NIPUNA stands out from the traditional classroom setting. By integrating workshops, seminars, technical and non-technical competitions, it offers an immersive learning experience. In the midst of this enriching environment, participants cultivate critical thinking, refine problem-solving skills, and gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge technology. Nipuna's events offer a distinctive and extraordinary experience, elevating them above the ordinary. Participants not only learn and collaborate but also engage in friendly competition across diverse events. Nipuna, as a visionary knowledge nexus, meticulously cultivates a culture of boundless creativity, ensuring the fluid dissemination of knowledge. This commitment contributes to the forging of academic pillars that sculpt revolutionary leaders and innovators set to leave an indelible mark on the future.
